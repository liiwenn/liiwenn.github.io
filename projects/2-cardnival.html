Card-nival

Project Type
School project
Fields
UI/UX Design, Graphic Design
Year
2020

Based on the project brief to do an E-commerce & Shopping app, I came up with the app prototype "Card-nival", an app that allows users to test play card games before they decide to buy the physical set.

The UI of the game was inspired from designs of cards which typically have a white stroke around it, implemented into the design patterns within the app.
